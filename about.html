Our Journey

Dale Roberts has been building custom wood furniture for over 10 years. These furniture are unique, contempory and fits perfectly in any space. In recent years he has been using Reclaimed wood in an effort to reuse, repurpose and recycle wood. This not only helps the environment but provide one of a kind rustic furniture.
